The Ministry of agriculture cooperation and farmers welfare has launched a two months programme commencing from 1 June 2018 to 31st July 2018 in 117 aspirational districts of the country. The program is called Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan and is focused on giving a boost to agriculture and allied activities in these aspirational districts. An action plan comprising of activities of the three departments DACFW DAHDF and DARE will be implemented during this period with Krishi Vigyan Kendras as the nodal point.
